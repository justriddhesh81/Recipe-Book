Egg Sandwich

Ingredients

Egg
Bread
Mayo
chilli powder
haldi
soya sauce

Preparation

you boil an egg, and then usko ek bowl mein daalo and start mashing it up with a spoon, midway usme ek spoon Mayo daalo, be careful to not put too much, we don't need too much. Fir usme, put ek pinch salt(i sometimes put salt, sometimes I don't so you could maybe say it's optional), tandoori masala(op), chilli powder, little sa haldi(very careful to put less coz Varna haldi ka kadwa taste aajayega), little sa soya sauce, chilli sauce... basically anything and everything you like. And mix it. It becomes a sort of paste yeah? Now toast the bread, i personally do it with butter. Aur ek slice pe i melt a slice of cheese. And then now it's time to assemble.
